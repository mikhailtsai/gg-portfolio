About me

My name is Georgii Gorshenin, and I'm a mobile game developer specializing in Unity. My passion for gaming started in my childhood, and I've always dreamt of being a part of this exciting industry. Though my journey into the world of IT took some time, I found my calling in game development.

My recent commercial work experience involves crafting simple hyper-casual games. I strive to create games from scratch, minimizing the use of plugins.

My goal is to thrive in the game development industry, gain experience, and create games that inspire and bring joy to players. I'm ready to take on any challenges and work on projects that allow me to bring my gaming dreams to life.
